Our Story

About Sprouts – The School

Sprouts – The School was founded with a simple but powerful belief: that the early years of a child's life are the most crucial. We built a preschool model that goes beyond academics — one that nurtures social, emotional, physical, and cognitive development through play-based, activity-driven learning.

Now, we're expanding our mission by inviting passionate entrepreneurs across India to join the Sprouts family and bring this premium preschool experience to their communities.

🔭
Our Vision
To become India's most trusted and loved preschool brand, where every child gets the best possible start in life.
🎯
Our Mission
To provide holistic, joyful, and high-quality early childhood education through a scalable franchise model across India.

Generally, investments range from setup costs, franchise fees, interiors, and working capital. We work to ensure the model is affordable and offers strong ROI potential.
No prior education experience is required! We provide complete training and operational support. What matters most is your passion for education, willingness to follow our system, and commitment to quality. Many of our successful partners come from business, IT, banking, and homemaker backgrounds.
We recommend a minimum of 1500 sq. ft. to a maximum of 3000 sq. ft. of space, ideally on the ground floor in a prime residential neighbourhood. Our infrastructure team will guide you on the ideal layout for classrooms, play areas, washrooms, and administrative spaces.
Typically, the setup process takes 60–90 days from agreement signing to grand launch. This includes interiors, branding, staff hiring, training, and the pre-launch marketing campaign. Our team is with you every step of the way to ensure a smooth and timely opening.
